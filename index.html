SoulPort Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

SoulPort ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

SoulPort connects you to your personal AI assistant. We collect only what is necessary to provide this service: messages you send, voice recordings you choose to submit, and basic device information for app functionality.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to deliver AI assistant responses. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Data Storage

Conversations are processed in real time and are not permanently stored beyond what is necessary for the assistant to function.
